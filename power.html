Off-Grid Solar Power System

welcome, my name is Genda Benjamin and since I live in an Off-Grid place with no electricity, I aim to design a small solar power system that will be affordable, reliable, and easy to maintain, suitable for powering basic electronics such as; my laptop, my small woofer and charge my phone.

System Overview

This system converts solar energy into usable AC power through regulated charging and safe energy storage. It is optimized for reliability, efficiency, and affordability.

Key Components

Solar Panel (18 V, 240 W)

Captures solar irradiance and converts it into DC electrical power using photovoltaic cells.

MPPT Charge Controller

Optimizes power transfer by dynamically matching panel output to battery charging requirements.

12 V 100 Ah Gel Battery

Stores energy safely and provides stable DC supply during low or no sunlight conditions.

Pure Sine Wave Inverter

Converts DC power into clean AC power suitable for sensitive electronic devices.

Safety & Design Considerations

  • Use DC breakers/fuses between battery and inverter
  • Ensure proper ventilation for battery and inverter
  • Correct cable sizing to prevent overheating
  • Fixed panel tilt of 30°–45° for optimal energy capture
